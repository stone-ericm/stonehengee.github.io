Biography

Born and raised in Queens, NY, Eric graduated from Queens High School for the Sciences, one of New York's prestigious Specialized High Schools. He then broke the mold and attended Wheaton College in small town Norton, MA, graduating with a degree in Political Science with a focus on political theory and minors in United State Economics and Medieval History. He took his talents to the political campaign world, most notably playing a major role in flipping the historically conservative Norton, MA Democratic for Congressman Joe Kennedy III. Eventually he realized that his skills could be better utilized in the tech world as a software developer, enrolling in an intensive bootcamp at Byte Academy in New York City. Since finishing the program he has pursued opportunities while spending his free time on passion projects.
